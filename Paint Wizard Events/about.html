About

Have you ever wanted to paint a picture entirely your own, but you’re not quite sure you have the skill? Maybe you’ve given painting a shot in the past, but you didn’t feel like you had the tools or practice to create something you really wanted to display in your home.Well, here at Paint Wizard, we can help you solve that.


Paint Wizard is an art and painting event unlike any other

When you sit down behind your easel your art ability doesn’t matter. When you take a Paint Wizard class our main focus will be on creating a unique, awesome painting you’ll be proud to show off — and have fun painting it.
Paint Wizard walks you through the step-by-step process of painting a picture — all while focusing on having a great time. By providing you with the building blocks you need to paint something you’ll love in a low-pressure setting, we put fun and simplicity into making art.
Paint Wizard classes are designed to be done as a group. Grab a few of your closest friends, order a glass of your favorite beer or wine, maybe some appetizers, and join us for a night of fun and creativity at Duffy’s in Redwood Falls, MN! Classes are taught by Steve Leske.
