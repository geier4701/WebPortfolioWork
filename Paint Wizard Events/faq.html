What is Paint Wizard?
Paint Wizard is a step-by-step painting class with a focus on fun. We create awesome paintings without the pressure — allowing you to sit back, relax, and learn a new skill!
I don't have any experience painting. Is Paint Wizard right for me?
Because Paint Wizard’s main goal is to have a great time, you don’t need to worry about your artistic skill! We welcome participants of any experience level.
How does Paint Wizard work?
If you’re interested in attending a Paint Wizard class, check out our calendar of events. Select the date that works best for you or choose your class based on the painting you’d like to create.

At each event, we focus on creating a unique painting. Your instructor will walk the class through the various steps to create the image, but don’t be scared to include your own creativity! These images are yours to keep, so make it your own!
How many people are in each Paint Wizard class?
We like to keep our Paint Wizard classes small. The minimum number of participants for a class is 6 and we’re comfortable taking on a class of 12. However, we do have the space for larger parties in special circumstances.
How much does a Paint Wizard ticket cost?
One ticket to a Paint Wizard event costs $35. This includes the cost of your canvas, paint, and brushes!
Where are Paint Wizard classes located?
We host each of our public Paint Wizard classes in the private room at Duffy's. But we can also come to your location for a private event!
